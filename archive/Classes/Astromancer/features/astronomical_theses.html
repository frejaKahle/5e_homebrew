2nd level astromancer feature

Starting at second level, you may choose from a list of theses to implement into your spellcasting. The number of theses you can implement is shown in the theses known column of the Astromancer Table.

Additionally, when you gain a level in this class, you can choose one of the theses you know and replace it with thesis that you could learn at that level.

If a thesis has Prerequisites, you must meet all of them to learn it. You can learn the thesis at the same time that you meet its Prerequisites. A level Prerequisite refers to your level in this class.

Farsight
Prerequisite: Wisdom spellcasting modifier

You become proficient in Wisdom (Perception).
When you cast a spell with a range of 20 feet or more, the range of that spell is increased by 30 feet.

Detailed Records
Prerequisite: Intelligence spellcasting modifier

You become proficient in Intelligence (History) checks and checks made to mimic handwriting of others. You can prepare two additional spells from the astromancer spell list.

Mountaintop Charting

You can hold your breath for 10 minutes and you gain a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

Sacrificial Spellcasting

When you cast an astromancer spell that targets a single creature and deals damage with at least three damage dice, you can choose to replace 2 of the spell's damage dice with damage equal to your spellcasting modifier of the type of one of the dice that was replaced.

  • If you make an attack roll as part of the spell, you gain a +2 bonus to the attack roll and you score a critical on a 19 or 20 on the d20.
  • If the target makes a saving throw as part of the spell, the target recieves a -2 modifier on its save. If the target fails its save by 3 or more, the next saving throw made by the target is made at disadvantage.

When you cast an astromancer spell that heals a single target that uses dice to determine the amount healed, you can forgo one healing die to allow the target to move up to half its movement.

Night Sight
Prerequisite: 6th level and Farsight thesis

You can rest while studying the stars, long rests require only 4 hours of sleep and 2 hours of light activity, such as stargazing.
You gain darkvision out to 60 feet or increase your range of darkvision by 60 feet if you already have a trait that grants darkvision.

Spell Proofs
Prerequisite: 6th level and Detailed Records thesis

At the end of a long rest, choose an astromancer spell you have prepared of 1st or second level. You can cast that spell once without using a spell slot. You
At 14th level, you can choose a spell of 3rd level or lower.

Oceanic Charting
Prerequisite: 6th level

You gain a swimming speed equal to your walking speed and you gain proficiency in Navigator's tools. When you make an ability check with navigator's tools, your proficiency bonus is doubled for the check. You gain advantage on perception checks that rely on sight.

Aura Power
Prerequisite: 10th level and have not taken the Aura Vitality or Aura Brightness theses

While your astronomical aura is active, your proficiency bonus is increased by 1.

Aura Vitality
Prerequisite: 10th level and have not taken the Aura Power or Aura Brightness theses

While your astronomical aura is active, you may choose to gain 5 temporary hit points at the start of each of your turns.
These temporary hit points do not stack and replace and temporary hit points you had.

Aura Brightness
Prerequisite: 10th level and have not taken the Aura Power or Aura Vitality theses

The bright and dim ranges of your astronomical aura are both doubled.
Additionally, The bright light from your aura counts as sunlight.

Fast Casting
Prerequisite: 14th level and have not taken the Sacrificial Spellcasting thesis

Choose one of the Astromancer Cantrips you know, you can cast that cantrip as a bonus action. You may change this selection when you prepare your spells as part of a long rest.

Skylight Connection
Prerequisite: 14th level

You gain a +2 bonus to all ability checks and saving throws while you can see the sky.
This bonus extends to all creatures of your choice within your astronomical aura.

Spatial Charting
Prerequisite: 14th level, Oceanic Charting theis, and Mountaintop Charting thesis

Your walking speed increases by 10 feet per turn and you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed.
You gain advantage on constitution saving throws made to resist exhaustion.

Mechanism Savant
Prerequisite: 18th level

You gain the ability to cast the 1st level spell attained from your Astronomical Mechanism at 1st level without using a spell slot.

Extraordinary Energy Source
Prerequisite: 18th level

You gain one additional spell slot for each of the following spell levels: 5th, 6th.
