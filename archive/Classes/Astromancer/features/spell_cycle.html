13th and 17th level Astromancer feature

At 13th level you gain the ability to recyle some of the energy from the cantrips you cast. You recieve the following abilities:

  • When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with an astromancer cantrip, roll a d20. On a roll of 8 through 15, you regain a 1st level spell slot. On roll of 16 through 20, you regain a spell slot of 2nd level.
  • When you use your Magic Mobility feature to move as you cast a spell, you can instead choose to teleport instead of walk the amount of feet granted by the feature.
At 17th level your ability to recycle the magical energy of your spells increases. You then gain the following abilities:
  • When you cast an astromancer cantrip on your turn for which you roll an attack roll against a single target, you may take a -5 penalty to the attack roll of the cantrip to target two seperate targets.
  • If you have more than 25 hit points, you can use the Cast a Spell action after the end of another creature's turn, with the limit that you can only cast a cantrip or spell of 5th level and below, or you can use a reaction provided you have already used your reaction on a particular round of combat.
    Doing so reduces you to one hit point, your hit point maximum is reduced by 25, and your level of Exhaustion is raised to 3. If you already have 3 levels of Exhaustion, you instead gain only 1 level of exhaustion.


You can use any of the above abilities a total number of times equal to your proficiency bonus.
