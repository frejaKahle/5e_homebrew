6th level Nebula object feature

Whenever you cast a conjuration spell of 3rd level or higher, your power cycle feature activates on a roll of 1 or 2 instead of just a roll of 1.

When you take fire damage or recieve damage as part of an evoaction spell, you may use your reaction reduce the spell's damage by 1d6 plus your proficiency bonus. If you reduce the damage below zero, you recieve a number of temporary hit points equal to the negative of the damage after being reduced.
