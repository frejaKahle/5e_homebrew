20th level Dwarf Star source feature

At 20th level, you tap into the depths of the dwarf star you draw your power from.
While your Astronomical Aura is active, if you are reduced to 0 hit points, you can choose to end the Astronomical Aura and stay at 1 hit point. All remaining uses of your Astronomical Aura are depeleted if you would have been killed by the original damage.

If you roll an 19 or higher on the d20 for a death saving throw you regain 1 hit point and become conscious, instantly ending the prone condition.
