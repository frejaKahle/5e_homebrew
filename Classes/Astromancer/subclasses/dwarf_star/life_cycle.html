6th level Dwarf Star source feature

Upon reaching 6th level, you gain the ability to use the excess energy of spells to heal your allies.
When you cast a spell of 3rd level or higher that heals at least one other creature and the roll of the die from your Power Cycle feature is a 1, you may choose to forgo regaining a spell slot. Istead, one creature of your choice that was targeted by the triggering spell regains additional hit points equal to twice your level in this class.
