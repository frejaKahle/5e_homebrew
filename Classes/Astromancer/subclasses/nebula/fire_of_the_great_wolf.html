20th level Nebula object feature

Whenever you cast a spell that has an area of effect, every dimension of the area of effect is doubled. Creatures that have a trait that allows them to take no damage on a successful dexterity saving throw or half damage on a failed dexterity saving throw take the normal amount of damage when you cast a spell with an area of effect.

Additionally, whenever you fail a death saving throw, a 5th level fireball spell detonates centered on you, but does not harm you or your allies.
This fireball is not affected by the aforementioned spell area of effect changes.
