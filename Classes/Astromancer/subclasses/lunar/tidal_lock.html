20th level Lunar object feature

You gain control over lunar effects on planetary oceans.
This feature has a number of charges equal to your proficiency bonus.
You regain all expended charges at the end of a long rest.
You may expend this feature's charges use to the following abilities:

Control Tides (1 charge): As an action, you cause ocean water to rise or fall 15 feet within a 300 foot radius of a point you can see for 1 hour. This water cannot flood onto land.

Create Current (1 charge): As a bonus action on your turn you choose a creature within 100 feet of you that you can see and is in water or as a reaction to a creature falling into water you target the creature that has fallen into water, you cause the target to be pushed with great force. The target must make a strength saving throw against your spell save DC, taking 2d8 force damage and moving 150 feet in a direction you choose on a failed save. On a successful save, the creature takes no damage and is moved only 50 feet.

Tsunami (2 charges): As an action, you cause a massive tidal wave to ravage an area, the wave of water crashes down on an are up to 60 feet long, 25 feet wide, and up to 20 feet tall. Each creature in that are must make a strength saving throw against your spell save DC + 2. On a failed save a creature takes 10d8 bludgeoning damage and is knocked Prone. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn't knocked Prone. The water then spreads out across the ground in all directions, extinguishing non-magical flames and magical fire from spells of 3rd level or below in its area and within 30 feet of it, and then vanishes.

Erosion (2 charges): As an action, you cause earth to erode from an area. You can remove up to 2500 cubic feet of dirt, stone, and vegetation. (20 five foot cubes may be removed this way)
