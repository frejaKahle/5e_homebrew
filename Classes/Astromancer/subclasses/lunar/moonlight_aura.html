14th level Lunar object feature

Shapechangers and creatures not in their original form by magical means that are within the bright light of your Astronomical Aura take 2d10 radiant damage and instantly revert to their original form at the beginning of each of your turns.
Illusions created from spells of 7th level or below instantly end upon entering the bright light of your Astronomical Aura, unless they were created by you or your allies.

While your astronomical aura is active, a Moonbeam spell you are concentrating on additionally targets a 5 foot wide line from you to the ceter of the cylinder and deals additional damage equal to 5 (1d10) plus your spellcasting modifier.
