6th level Singularity object feature

At 6th level, you gain the ability to alter the space around you. As a reaction to an attack against a target within 30 feet of you, before or after the result of the attack is known, you can impose a penalty against the attack roll equal to your proficiency bonus.
Additionally, you can alter the course of some magic. Whenever a spell that targets an area targets an area within 30 feet of you, you can use a reaction to shift the spell's affected area by 5 ft.
