2nd level astromancer feature

Starting at second level, you may choose from a list of theses to implement into your spellcasting. The number of theses you can implement is shown in the theses known column of the Astromancer Table.

Additionally, when you gain a level in this class, you can choose one of the theses you know and replace it with thesis that you could learn at that level.

If a thesis has Prerequisites, you must meet them to learn it. You can learn the thesis at the same time that you meet its Prerequisites. A level Prerequisite refers to your level in this class.

Farsight
Prerequisite: Wisdom spellcasting modifier

You become proficient in Wisdom (Perception), if you are already proficient in Wisdom (Perception), you gain double proficiency (Expertise) in Wisdom (Perception) checks.
When you cast a spell with a range greater than 20 feet, the range of the spell is increased by 30 feet.

Detailed Records
Prerequisite: Intelligence spellcasting modifier

You become proficient in Intelligence (History) checks and checks made to mimic handwriting of others. You can prepare two additional spells from the astromancer spell list.

Scholarly Cowardice

You can take the Disengage action as a bonus act on on your turn.

Graveyard Shift

You have advantage on saving throws and ability checks made to resist exhaustion.
You gain proficiency in Stealth (Dexterity) checks.

Elevated Charting

You can hold your breath for 30 minutes underwater and you gain a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

Stargazing
Prerequisite: 6th level and Wisom spellcasting modifier

You can rest while studying the stars, long rests require only 4 hours of sleep and 2 hours of light activity, such as stargazing.
When you take a short rest, after rolling hit dice, you may roll an additional d6 and add the result to your health.

Spell Proofs
Prerequisite: 6th level and Intelligence spellcasting modifier

At the end of a long rest, you can magically create a spell scroll of a spell you have prepared of 1st or 2nd level without consuming a spell slot.
At 14th level, you can create a spell scroll of up to 3rd level that is in the astromancer spell list.

Heightened Cycling
Prerequisite: 6th level

You can choose to forgo two dice from a spell damage or healing roll for your Spell Cycle Feature.
Additionally, you can choose to use one of your saved dice to a spell attack roll or a to your spell save DC for a single spell.

Aura Power
Prerequisite: 10th level and have not taken the Aura Vitality or Aura Brightness theses

While your astronomical aura is active, your proficiency bonus is increased by 1.

Aura Vitality
Prerequisite: 10th level and have not taken the Aura Power or Aura Brightness theses

While your astronomical aura is active, you may choose to gain 5 temporary hit points at the start of each of your turns.
These temporary hit points do not stack and replace and temporary hit points you had.

Aura Brightness
Prerequisite: 10th level and have not taken the Aura Power or Aura Vitality theses

The bright and dim ranges of your astronomical aura are both doubled.
Additionally, The bright light from your aura counts as sunlight.

Fast Casting
Prerequisite: 14th level

Choose one of the Astromancer Cantrips you know, you can cast that cantrip as a bonus action. You may change this selection when you prepare your spells as part of a long rest.

Skylight Connection
Prerequisite: 14th level

You gain a +2 bonus to all ability checks and saving throws while you can see the sky.

Navigation Master
Prerequisite: 14th level

Your proficiency bonus is doubled (Expertise) for ability checks made with Navigator's Tools. You gain advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks.

Magic Salvage
Prerequisite: 18th level

You gain the ability to cast the 1st level spell attained from your Astronomical Mechanism at 1st level without using a spell slot.

Extra Magical Capabilities
Prerequisite: 18th level

You gain one additional spell slot for each of the following spell levels: 5th, 6th, 7th, 8th.
