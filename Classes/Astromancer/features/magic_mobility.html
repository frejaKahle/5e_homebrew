5th, 9th, 13th, and 17th level astromancer feature

Starting at 5th level, you gain the ability to move when you cast spells.
You can walk up to five times the spell's level in feet as part of the Cast a Spell action if the spell you cast is an astromancer spell of first level or higher.

At 9th level, your walking speed increases by 10 feet if you are not wearing armor or holding a shield and you are holding an astromancer spellcasting focus.

At 17th level, your walking speed increases again by 10 feet, for a total bonus of 20 ft if you are not wearing armor or holding a shield andyou are holding an astromancer spellcasting focus.
