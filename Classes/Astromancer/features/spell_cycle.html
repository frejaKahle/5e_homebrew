13th level Astromancer feature

At 13th level you gain the ability to recyle some of the energy from the cantrips you cast.
When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with an astromancer cantrip, roll a d20. On a roll of 8 through 15, you regain a 1st level spell slot. On roll of 16 through 20, you regain a spell slot of 2nd level.
